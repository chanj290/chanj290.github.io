Entering the MTR Station

As you walk to the MTR exit to enter the MTR station, you pass by buskers on the street, singing their hearts out as a couple bystanders stay to watch.

You enter the MTR exit, and walk down a flight of stairs. As you enter the concourse, a flood of MTR station ambience noises surround you.

You notice a line of shops that await you within the station:

  • Hang Seng Bank ATM
  • Hana-Musubi Onigiri Shop
  • 7-Eleven
  • Arome Bakery
  • Pret A Manger
  • Mrs. Field's Cookies
Proceed to the turnstiles.
