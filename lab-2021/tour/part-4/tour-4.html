MTR Platform

MTR Staff are posted throughout the station to help commuters get around.

One of them points you in the direction of the escalator to get to the train platform.

Commuters shuffle behind each other to form a single file line and stand on the right hand side of the escalator.

You listen for the announcement to play as they tell you when the next train is arriving.

Fun Fact

You line up behind other commuters to enter the train doors. A neat line on both the left and right of each train door.

The train is arriving!
